Who implements the decision of ministry?

The Indian Bureaucracy today is an enormously complex system. It consist of the All-India services, state services, employees of the local governments, technical and managerial staff running public sector undertakings. Union Public Service Commision has been entrusted with task of conducting the process of recruitment of the Civil servants for the government of India. The members of the civil services are selected for the fixed term. Their removal or the suspension is subject to a thorough enquiry made by the judge of the Supreme Court. The persons selected by the UPSC for Indian Administrative Service and Indian Police Service constitute the backbone of the higher level bureaucracy in the States. Collector of the district is the most important officer of the Government at District level.
